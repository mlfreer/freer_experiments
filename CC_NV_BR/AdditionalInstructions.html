New Stage:

  • After seeing the outcome of Stage 2, you have the opportunity to revise the number of decals you purchased in Stage 2.
  • You can increase, decrease or keep the same number of decals.
  • After you and the other 3 participants made their revisions, the computer will randomly choose to implement the revision of one of the 4 participants.
  • This means, in every round, there is a 25% chance that the final outcome table will include your revised choice, but not those of the other 3 participants; their choices would be unchanged.
  • You will then move to the next round.
