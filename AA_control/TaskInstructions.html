After counting the number of zeros in the table, you have to enter the number into the box on the right side of the screen. After you have entered your answer, you will need to click the Validate button. Once you have clicked the button, a new table will be generated. A new table will be generated regardless of whether the number of zeros you have entered was correct or not.
