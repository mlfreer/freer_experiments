Structure of a Round

  • You will be randomly assigned to a group of four participants each round.
  • You and the other group members will compete in a tournament.
  • Each round of the experiment consists of three stages:
    1. Effort Stage
    2. Investment Stage
    3. Tournament Outcome Stage
  • We now describe the three stages in more detail.

Effort Stage

  • You will see a set of sliders on your screen.
    • You can adjust each slider to any position between 0 and 100 by pressing the slider with your mouse and dragging it to the desired position.
  • You have one minute to adjust as many sliders as possible to the target position of 50.
  • You will receive one token for each correctly adjusted slider.
  • At the end of the experiment, tokens will be converted to pounds at the following exchange rate:
    1 token = 50p.

Investment Stage

  • You decide how many of your previously earned tokens to keep and how many of your tokens to invest in training.
  • Training determines your performance in the tournament.
  • Each token invested in training increases your tournament score by one point.
  • For example, if you received 21 tokens from the task and invested 10 tokens in training, then your tournament score is 10 and you will be paid 11 tokens if the round is chosen for payment.

Tournament Outcome Stage

  • Once everyone has concluded with their investment you will see the rank you achieved in the tournament along with usernames, investments, and ranks for other group members as on the screenshot below.
  • The outcome stage contains your rank, username, and investment .

Timeline of an Experimental Round

  1. At the beginning of each round, you are randomly assigned to a group of four participants who will compete with each other in a tournament.
  2. In the Effort Stage, you have one minute to adjust as many sliders as possible to the target position of 50, and you earn one token for each correctly adjusted slider .
  3. In the Investment Stage, you decide how many of your earned tokens to keep and how many tokens to invest in training. Training determines your performance in the tournament . Each token invested increases your tournament score by one point.
  4. In the Tournament Outcome Stage, your tournament score will be compared to the scores of other group members . Your ranking will be shown, from 1 (highest score) to 4 (lowest score).
Then you proceed to the next round. At the end of the experiment, one round is randomly selected for payment. You receive 50p for each token you have at the end of that round.

Payment:

  • Once the experiment is finished one of the rounds would be randomly selected for payment.
  • All rounds are equally likely to be selected thus please take them seriously.
  • All payments are conducted electronically using the Tremendous system.
